about



Hi, my name is Nancy Tran. I'm a student at Northeastern University-Seattle, where I am pursuing a Master's in Computer Science-Align. What's unique about that Align program is that it's catered for students who had previously studied a different subject. Previously, I received my Bachelor's of Science in Biochemistry at Bates College. You can check out this publication, containing part of my thesis. After undergrad, I had intended to go into biomedical research, which led me to spend 2 years at the University of Washington researching genes involved in hair cell regeneration in zebrafish. In the mist of finding out that the pace of research wasn't for me, I also discovered my joy in computer programming. This has brought me where I am today.

This website is a portfolio of my coursework for CS7580: Special Topics in Software Engineering at Northeastern University-Seattle.
